SUBSCRIBER SEGMENTATION

This feature allows you to automatically segment your subscribers according to the content type and category of the page they subscribed from.

After adding segmentation to your web pages, when a user subscribes to the push notifications for your website, he will be automatically market and segmented.

For example:

Different pages on your website (f.e: shoe page, bags page, shirts page etc.) can be segmented with the "Segment Type": Fashion. Using the "Segment Name" will allow you to differentiate between the pages.

Subscriber segmentation will allow you to send targeted notifications to your website subscribers; f.e to all the subscribers who segmented as "Fashion" (Segment Type) and/or send a specific campaign only to subscribers who arrived from the "shoe page" (Segment Name).
