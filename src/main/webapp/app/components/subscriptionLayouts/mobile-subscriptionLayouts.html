MOBILE SUBSCRIPTION LAYOUTS

Customize your subscription layout window (Permission Message) for web push notifications.
Promotion message will be shown on your website and will ask the users to give your website a permission to send to them push notifications. Without this permission, users will not be able to successfully subscribe to your website and receive notifications.
Customized promotion messages improve the user experience of your website and increase the likelihood that users will want to receive notifications from your website.
Mobile subscription Layout will be shown on your website for all users who are browsing your website using a mobile device. You can set up a specific subscription layout for mobile and a different one for desktop.
